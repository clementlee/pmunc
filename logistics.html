Please email your Rooming List directly to Michaela Jimenez, the Convention Service Manager. All initial rooming lists are due by Wednesday, October 30, 2014. Reservation changes may be submitted after the due date via phone and/or email. If you have any other questions, please contact Michaela at 609-734-4056, or email her at michaela.jimenez@hyatt.com.

If your organization is Tax Exempt for New Jersey; please email (michaela.jimenez@hyatt.com) or fax (609-734-4051) your ST-5 Form for N.J. to Michaela Jimenez. Some other important information about taxes includes:

  • In the state of New Jersey, 2 types of taxes apply for guest room charges: 7% N.J. Sales tax and 8% Occupancy tax
  • The ST-5 Form will exempt you from the 7% N.J. Sales Tax, but your organization will still be responsible for the 8% Occupancy tax.
  • Please note that the organization name on the Tax Exempt Form and the Payment Method (Credit/Company Check/Certified Bank Check) must match.


Payment



You can pay for the guestrooms by a Credit Card, Certified Bank Check or Company Check.

Credit Card

If you choose this option, you will be required to fill out a credit card authorization form and provide a copy of the credit card. Please included the form and credit card copy when submitting the initial Rooming List on Wednesday, October 30, 2014.

Certified Bank Check or Company Check

If you choose this option, your check must be received by the hotel no later than Friday, November 7, 2014.


Meals


PMUNC 2014 provides three dining options for the conference:

Hyatt Regency Meal Plan

The hotel has put together a meal plan, for $92.00 (inclusive of all taxes and service fees), which includes two lunches (on Saturday and Sunday) and three dinners (on Thursday, Friday and Saturday). Vegetarian options will be available. We strongly encourage you to purchase this plan and enjoy the diverse options that the Hyatt Regency chefs provide!

Breakfast

Delegations can elect to pay $112.00 (includes all taxes and service charges) for the aforementioned 5-meal plan, with an additional continental breakfast provided on Friday, Saturday and Sunday.

Delivery

Several nearby restaurants offer delivery services. If you choose this option, we strongly encourage you to order your food before committee session ends, as it may not reach in time if ordered afterwards.


Driving Directions


From the North

Take the NJ Turnpike South to Exit 9 (New Brunswick). After toll, bear right onto Route 18 North; go 1/2 mile, then Take Route 1 South. Follow Route 1 South for 18 miles to Princeton. Take Alexander Road East Exit. At second light, make a right. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From the South (New Jersey)

Take I-295 North to Exit 67A (Route 1 North). Follow Route 1 North for 3 miles to Princeton/Alexander Road Exit. Make a right at light. At next light, make a right. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From the Southwest (Pennsylvania)

Take the Pennsylvania Turnpike to Route 1 North Exit. Follow Route 1 North to I-95 North into New Jersey. From I-95 take Exit 67 (Route 1 North). Follow Route 1 North approximately 3 miles to Princeton/Alexander Road. Make a right at light. At next light, make a right. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From the Northwest

Take I-287 to Route 206 South into Princeton. At the intersection of Route 206 and Nassau Street, make a left onto Nassau Street (Route 27). Make a right onto University Place (first traffic light). Follow to traffic light and make a left onto Alexander Road. Follow for approximately 3 miles. At third light, make a right. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From the East

Take Route 33 West to Route 133 West To Route 571 West. Follow Route 571 West for approximately 11 miles to intersection of Route 571 and Route 1. At light, make left onto Route 1 South. Get in right lane to take Alexander Road East Exit. At second light, make a right. Hotel entrance is on the right.
