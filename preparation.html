Awards Policy


The following four tiers of awards may be given at PMUNC for each committee:
  • Best Delegate or Delegation
  • Outstanding Delegate or Delegation
  • Honorable Mention
  • Verbal Commendation
Awards will be given on the basis of the following criteria, in no particular order:
  • Adherence to policy of country or character
  • Public speaking ability
  • Ability to negotiate, compromise and form effective agreements
  • Position papers (where applicable)
  • Knowledge of the subject material
  • Knowledge and proper application of the Rules of Procedure
  • Professional and proper conduct
  • Adherence to PMUNC policies (attendance, dress code, etc.)
PMUNC reserves the right to keep all of its judging proceedings confidential. Delegates who failed to comply with the PMUNC Code of Conduct, Conference rules or Hotel rules may, at the Secretary-General's discretion, be disqualified for awards.
In addition to the Committee awards, four school awards will be given for the first and second-place schools, both in the large and small school categories. The school awards will be based on a variety of factors, including the school's overall performance and conduct, delegation size and the overall effort put into the Conference.
